Your Trusted Home Improvement Partner in Germantown

Germantown is one of Montgomery County's largest and fastest-growing communities, stretching along the I-270 corridor with a mix of newer planned neighborhoods, townhome communities, and single-family subdivisions built primarily from the 1980s through the 2010s. Many of these homes are now reaching the 15- to 30-year mark — the point where composite decks start showing wear, builder-grade bathrooms feel dated, and the routine maintenance that was once optional becomes essential. Leo General Contracting understands the specific needs of Germantown homes at this stage of their lifecycle, delivering practical upgrades and reliable repairs that protect your investment and modernize your living space without unnecessary disruption.

What We Offer

Home Improvement Services in Germantown

Germantown's newer-but-aging developments create steady demand for targeted home improvements. Our deck and carpentry team handles everything from resurfacing weathered decks and replacing failing railings to building new outdoor living spaces — work that is especially common in neighborhoods like Clopper's Mill and Century Oak where original decks have reached the end of their lifespan. Our kitchen and bathroom refresh service is another top request, helping homeowners swap out builder-grade vanities, install modern tile backsplashes, and update fixtures without the cost and timeline of a full gut remodel.

For the ongoing upkeep that keeps Germantown homes in top condition, our handyman services cover everything from drywall patching and door adjustments to fixture installs and minor plumbing repairs — all handled in a single visit. And with many Germantown properties sitting on quarter-acre lots with mature landscaping, our landscaping and yard maintenance team provides mulching, garden bed restoration, seasonal cleanups, and the curb appeal work that keeps your property looking sharp year-round.
