Local Expertise

Your Trusted Home Improvement Partner in Olney

Olney sits in the northern part of Montgomery County with a distinctly suburban, family-oriented character. The community is known for its established single-family neighborhoods, many built in the 1970s through 1990s, set among rolling terrain and mature tree cover. Homes here range from traditional colonials and split-levels to larger custom builds on wooded lots, and that variety creates demand for a broad range of home improvement work. Leo General Contracting understands what Olney homeowners need — whether it is refreshing dated interiors with new paint and tile, rebuilding a weathered deck overlooking a tree-lined backyard, or tackling the accumulated repair list that comes with owning a well-loved home in an established neighborhood.

What We Offer

Home Improvement Services in Olney

Olney's established single-family homes — many now 30 to 50 years old — frequently need the kind of updates that make a real difference in daily living. Our interior and exterior painting crews handle everything from refreshing dated wood paneling and popcorn-ceiling rooms to recoating weathered siding and shutters on split-levels and colonials. For outdoor living spaces, our deck and carpentry team builds new composite and wood decks, replaces rotting deck boards, installs railings, and handles finish carpentry like crown molding and built-in shelving that updates interiors without a full renovation.

With mature yards and larger lots throughout the community, landscaping and yard maintenance is another area where Olney homeowners turn to us for seasonal cleanups, mulch installation, and garden bed work that keeps properties looking their best. Inside the home, our tile work service addresses aging bathroom floors, outdated shower surrounds, and kitchen backsplashes — the kind of targeted improvements that modernize a space without the cost or disruption of a full remodel.
