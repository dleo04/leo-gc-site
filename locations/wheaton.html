What We Offer

Home Improvement Services in Wheaton

Wheaton's older homes benefit most from practical, well-executed maintenance work. Our general home repairs address the issues that come with aging construction — sticking doors, drafty windows, worn caulking, and damaged trim. For homeowners looking to refresh tired interiors or protect weathered exteriors, our interior and exterior painting service delivers clean prep work, quality paints, and a lasting finish that transforms rooms and curb appeal alike.

We also handle the drywall repair that mid-century homes so often need — patching cracks, skim coating damaged walls, and matching existing textures so repairs blend seamlessly. And for the steady stream of smaller tasks that keep a home running smoothly, our handyman services cover everything from fixture replacements and shelf installs to door hardware swaps and light carpentry, all handled in a single visit.
