About This Service

Seamless Drywall Work for Every Situation

Drywall damage is one of the most common issues homeowners face — from doorknob dents and nail pops to plumbing leaks that leave ugly water stains and crumbling surfaces. The challenge isn't just fixing the hole; it's making the repair completely invisible against the surrounding wall. That's where experience matters, and it's what Leo General Contracting brings to every drywall project in Montgomery County.

We handle everything from hanging full sheets in new construction and basement finishing to precision patching and skim coating. Whether your Gaithersburg townhome has a fist-sized hole behind the door or your Silver Spring basement needs complete drywall finishing after framing, we match the existing texture and leave you with walls that look factory-smooth.
