Home Repair Questions & Answers

No minimum job size. Whether it's a single door that won't close properly or a list of ten small repairs around the house, we're happy to help. We provide a clear estimate upfront so you know exactly what each item costs. Many homeowners save money by bundling several small repairs into one visit.
We do our best to accommodate urgent situations. If you have a broken window, a door that won't lock, or a leak that's causing active damage, call us and we'll get there as quickly as our schedule allows. For true plumbing or electrical emergencies, you may need a licensed specialist first — we can help coordinate and handle the repair work after.
Pretty much anything on a homeowner's fix-it list that doesn't require a licensed electrician or plumber. Door adjustments, window repairs, drywall touch-ups, caulking, fixture swaps, shelf mounting, railing repairs, weatherstripping, floor squeaks — if you're not sure whether something falls under general repair, just call and ask. We'll let you know honestly.
For most repair jobs, we can typically schedule a visit within a few days. Smaller repairs often get completed the same day we come to estimate. Availability varies by season — spring and summer are our busiest times — so calling sooner gives you more flexibility in scheduling.
