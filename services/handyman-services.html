About This Service

The Broad-Scope Fix-It Service for Montgomery County Homes

Most homeowners don't need a specialist for every small job — they need someone skilled, dependable, and willing to tackle a list of mixed tasks in a single visit. That's exactly what our handyman service delivers. We show up with a loaded truck, assess each item, and work through your list efficiently so you're not scheduling four different contractors for four different problems.

A wobbly ceiling fan in your Bethesda colonial, a sticking patio door in your Rockville split-level, a bathroom towel bar that pulled out of the drywall in your Potomac home — these are the kinds of tasks we handle every single day. We bring the tools, the know-how, and the materials to get it done in one trip whenever possible. No job is too small and no list is too long.
