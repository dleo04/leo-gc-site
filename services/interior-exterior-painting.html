About This Service

Professional Painting That Protects and Beautifies Your Home

A quality paint job does more than change the color of your walls — it protects your home's surfaces from moisture, wear, and weather damage while completely refreshing the look and feel of your living space. At Leo General Contracting, every painting project starts with thorough surface preparation because we know that a lasting finish depends on what happens before the first coat goes on.

Whether you need a single accent wall refreshed in your Potomac dining room, every room repainted in your Rockville colonial, or the entire exterior of your Bethesda home protected with a durable new finish, we bring the same attention to detail. We tape, prime, and apply every coat with care — then clean up as if we were never there.

Painting Questions & Answers

Most interior rooms can be completed in 1–2 days, depending on size and prep work needed. A full interior repaint of a typical Montgomery County home takes 3–5 days. Exterior projects usually run 3–7 days depending on the home's size, siding material, and weather conditions. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free estimate.
We use premium-grade paints from trusted brands like Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams. For interiors, we typically recommend low-VOC formulas for a healthier home environment. For exteriors, we select products rated for Maryland's humidity and temperature swings. We're happy to discuss specific finishes and sheen levels during your consultation.

Interior painting can be done year-round. Exterior painting in Maryland is best scheduled between late spring and early fall when temperatures stay consistently above 50°F. If you have an exterior project in mind, we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred dates during the prime painting season.
