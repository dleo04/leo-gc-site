About This Service

Transform Your Kitchen Without a Full Remodel

Your kitchen is the heart of your home, and it doesn't need a complete tear-out to feel new again. Cabinet refacing or a fresh coat of paint, updated hardware, a modern backsplash, and new countertops can completely transform the look and feel of a dated kitchen. Many Montgomery County homeowners are amazed by how much impact these targeted updates deliver — often at a fraction of a full renovation budget.

Leo General Contracting handles every aspect of your kitchen refresh, from selecting the right countertop material and laying precise backsplash tile to installing under-cabinet lighting and upgrading your sink and faucet. Whether you're updating a galley kitchen in your Bethesda colonial or refreshing the open-concept kitchen in your Potomac home, we bring meticulous craftsmanship and efficient scheduling to every project so your kitchen is back in service quickly.

Kitchen Refresh Questions

A kitchen refresh typically costs a fraction of a full remodel because we work within your existing layout and avoid major structural changes, plumbing rerouting, or electrical rewiring. By focusing on high-impact updates — cabinet refacing, new countertops, backsplash tile, and updated fixtures — you get a dramatically transformed kitchen without the $50K+ price tag of a gut renovation. We'll provide a detailed estimate tailored to your specific goals during your free consultation.
A straightforward kitchen refresh — cabinet painting, hardware swap, and new fixtures — can be completed in 2–3 days. A more involved project with countertop replacement, backsplash tile, and lighting updates typically takes 4–7 days.
