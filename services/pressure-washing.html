About This Service

Restore Your Home's Curb Appeal

Montgomery County's humid mid-Atlantic climate creates the perfect conditions for mold, mildew, algae, and organic staining to take hold on your home's exterior surfaces. Over time, your siding loses its color, driveways and walkways develop dark streaks, decks turn gray and slippery, and gutters show ugly black tiger stripes. Professional pressure washing removes all of that buildup — from house siding and brick to concrete driveways, flagstone walkways, wooden decks, patio pavers, fence lines, and gutter exteriors — bringing every surface back to its original appearance.

Not every surface can handle the same water pressure. That's why Leo General Contracting uses both high-pressure washing for durable surfaces like concrete and brick, and low-pressure soft washing for more delicate materials like vinyl siding, painted wood, stucco, and roof shingles. Soft washing relies on specialized cleaning solutions that break down organic growth at the root, so surfaces stay cleaner longer without risk of damage. Whether you need a full house wash before painting, a driveway cleaning after winter, or a deck restoration before summer entertaining, we match the right technique to every surface on your property.

Pressure Washing Questions

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt and stains from hard surfaces like concrete driveways, brick, and stone. Soft washing uses a much lower water pressure combined with specialized biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely remove mold, mildew, and algae from delicate surfaces like vinyl siding, painted wood, stucco, and roof shingles. We assess every surface on your property and use the appropriate method to get the best results without any risk of damage.
For most Montgomery County homes, we recommend a full exterior wash once a year. Driveways and walkways may benefit from cleaning every 1–2 years depending on tree cover and shade. Homes surrounded by mature trees or in heavily shaded areas tend to accumulate mold and algae faster and may need attention more frequently. An annual wash keeps surfaces looking fresh and prevents long-term staining that becomes harder to remove over time.

We use soft washing with low pressure and cleaning solutions for vinyl siding, HardiePlank, painted surfaces, and other delicate materials. High-pressure washing is reserved for durable surfaces like concrete and natural stone. Our crew is trained to recognize different siding types and adjust their approach accordingly, so your home's exterior is cleaned thoroughly without any damage.
Spring and early fall are the ideal times. Spring washing removes the grime, pollen, and organic growth that built up over winter, getting your home ready for summer. Fall cleaning clears away summer buildup before the wet winter months set in. That said, we offer pressure washing throughout the warmer months — from April through November — whenever temperatures are consistently above freezing. If you're planning to paint or stain, pressure washing should be done 2–3 days before the new finish is applied.
