About This Service

Making Homes Show-Ready Before They Hit the Market

In Montgomery County's competitive real estate market, first impressions drive offers. A scuffed wall, a dripping faucet, or dated cabinet hardware can cost sellers thousands in perceived value — and those are exactly the kinds of issues buyers' inspectors flag. Leo General Contracting works with listing agents and homeowners to address every repair and cosmetic update before the photographer arrives, so the home presents at its absolute best from day one on the MLS.

We understand the urgency of real estate timelines. When a listing date is set, there's no room for delays. That's why we offer priority scheduling for listing prep projects, provide fast-turnaround estimates, and coordinate multi-trade work so painting, drywall, fixture swaps, and touch-ups happen in the right sequence without wasted days. Whether you're an agent preparing a Chevy Chase estate or a seller getting your Gaithersburg townhome ready, we deliver on deadline.

For Real Estate Agents: We work directly with agents as a reliable repair partner for your clients. One point of contact, fast quotes, and consistent quality across listings. Call us to discuss a standing arrangement for your active inventory.
